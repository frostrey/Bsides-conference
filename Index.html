About the BSides Conference

The BSides Conference is an annual event that brings together cybersecurity professionals, hackers, researchers, and enthusiasts from Kenya. It serves as a platform for sharing the latest insights, trends, and innovations in the field of cybersecurity. The event features a diverse range of talks, hands-on workshops, and panel discussions, each led by experts in the industry. Participants have the opportunity to deepen their technical knowledge, enhance their skills, and engage in meaningful networking with like-minded individuals. With its collaborative and inclusive atmosphere, BSides plays a crucial role in fostering learning, growth, and awareness within the global cybersecurity community.
